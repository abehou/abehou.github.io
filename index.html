Hi! I am Abe Hou.

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at Stanford University, working with Chris Manning, Diyi Yang, and Dan Ho.

I am interested in making language models work in non-verifiable domains, human–AI collaboration, and social simulation.

I graduated from Johns Hopkins University with a B.S. in Computer Science and Mathematics. I am extremely grateful to have worked with Ben Van Durme, Daniel Khashabi, and Tianxing He.
